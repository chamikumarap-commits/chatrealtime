Community Rules

These rules exist to keep ChatNow a welcoming, safe, and enjoyable space for everyone. Breaking them has consequences.

Last updated: January 1, 2026 Enforced site-wide

These rules apply to all users, in all areas of ChatNow — Random Chat, Chat Rooms, and Direct Messages. Violations may result in immediate removal, temporary bans, or permanent blocks. Serious violations will be reported to law enforcement.

Rule 1. You Must Be 18 or Older

ChatNow is an adult platform. You must be at least 18 years old to use any part of this service. If we suspect a user is underage, their access will be terminated immediately. If you encounter a user you believe is a minor, report them at once.

Rule 2. Treat Every Person With Respect

Behind every chat is a real human being. Treat them with basic dignity. This means:

  • No insults, personal attacks, or targeted harassment
  • No bullying, mocking, or deliberately making someone feel bad
  • No threatening language of any kind
  • No persistent messaging after someone has clearly asked you to stop

Disagreements happen — that's fine. But they must remain respectful. If you can't have a respectful conversation with someone, disconnect and move on.

Rule 3. Zero Tolerance for Content Involving Minors

This is our strictest rule and there are absolutely no exceptions. The following are immediately actionable offences that will result in a permanent ban and a report to law enforcement and NCMEC:

  • Any sexual or suggestive content involving anyone under 18
  • Soliciting minors for sexual conversations or images
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting CSAM of any kind
  • Grooming behaviour directed at anyone under 18

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies worldwide on any reports of this nature.

Rule 4. No Hate Speech or Discrimination

Content that dehumanises people based on who they are is not welcome here. Prohibited hate speech includes:

Racism or racial slurs
Religious intolerance or bigotry
Homophobic or transphobic language
Sexist or misogynistic content
Xenophobia or nationalism that dehumanises others
Disability-based mockery or discrimination

Rule 5. No Spam, Bots, or Advertising

ChatNow is a place for real human conversations, not promotions. You may not:

  • Flood chats with repeated identical or similar messages
  • Use automated bots or scripts to interact with users
  • Advertise products, services, websites, or social media accounts
  • Solicit users for OnlyFans, paid platforms, or subscription services
  • Send unsolicited links, especially shortened or suspicious ones

Rule 6. No Scams, Fraud, or Financial Exploitation

Attempting to defraud or financially exploit other users is strictly prohibited. This includes romance scams, fake emergency stories, crypto or investment schemes, phishing links, and any request for money, gift cards, or valuables from users you have met on the platform.

Rule 7. No Illegal Content or Activities

ChatNow must not be used to facilitate illegal activity of any kind. Examples include but are not limited to:

  • Drug sales or solicitation
  • Human trafficking or exploitation
  • Weapons sales or illegal firearms discussion
  • Doxxing — sharing someone's private personal information without consent
  • Non-consensual intimate image sharing (NCII / "revenge porn")
  • Impersonating law enforcement, ChatNow staff, or other users

Rule 8. No Impersonation

You may choose any nickname, but you may not impersonate a real person, a public figure, a ChatNow staff member, or another user. Creating a false identity designed to deceive others is prohibited.

Enforcement & Consequences

We use a combination of automated systems, community reports, and human review to enforce these rules. Consequences scale with severity:

  • Minor violations: Warning or session disconnect
  • Moderate violations: Temporary suspension from the platform
  • Severe violations: Permanent IP-level ban
  • Criminal violations: Immediate permanent ban + report to law enforcement and relevant authorities

Because chat sessions are not stored, we rely heavily on real-time reports. Please use the in-chat Report button whenever you see a violation. Your report is anonymous and helps protect the entire community.
