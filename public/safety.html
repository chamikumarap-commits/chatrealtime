Short version: ChatNow does not store your chat messages, does not require an account, and does not sell your data. We collect only the minimum technical information needed to run the service.

1. Overview

ChatNow ("we", "us", "our") operates the ChatNow platform available at chatnow.com and associated subdomains (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By using ChatNow, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following minimal information to operate the Service:

2.1 Automatically Collected Technical Data

  • IP Address — collected temporarily for abuse prevention and rate limiting. Not linked to identity. Purged within 24 hours.
  • Browser & Device Type — collected in aggregate to optimise compatibility (e.g., Chrome 121, iOS 17). No fingerprinting.
  • Session Identifiers — a temporary UUID assigned to your connection. Destroyed when you disconnect. Not persistent.
  • Connection Timestamps — time of connect/disconnect stored for 48 hours for server monitoring.

2.2 User-Provided Data

  • Nickname — the display name you choose. Not verified, not stored after your session ends.
  • Gender, Age Range, Country — optional fields used for matching. Stored in your browser session only, never on our servers.
  • Contact Form Submissions — name, email, and message when you email us or submit our contact form. Retained for up to 12 months to resolve your enquiry.

2.3 Chat Content

Chat messages (text and images) are transmitted in real-time via encrypted WebSocket connections and are never stored on our servers. We have no ability to retrieve, review, or produce past chat conversations.

4. How We Use Your Data

The limited data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Operate and maintain the real-time chat service
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and violations of our Terms & Conditions
  • Monitor server performance and diagnose technical issues
  • Respond to enquiries submitted via our contact form
  • Comply with applicable law and legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Infrastructure providers — our servers are hosted on reputable cloud providers (e.g., Render, AWS). They process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements and do not use it for their own purposes.
  • Law enforcement — we may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ChatNow, our users, or the public. Given our minimal data retention, what we can provide is extremely limited.
  • Business transfer — if ChatNow is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may be transferred. We will notify users via a prominent notice on the Service.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

ChatNow uses sessionStorage (browser memory cleared when the tab closes) to store your nickname and preferences for a single session. We do not use persistent tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

We use one functional cookie: a session token to keep your connection authenticated during a visit. This cookie expires when you close your browser.

You can clear cookies and session data at any time through your browser settings.

8. Security

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. WebSocket connections use WSS (WebSocket Secure). We employ industry-standard security practices including:

  • Encrypted data in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse and DDoS
  • Regular security audits
  • No storage of sensitive personal data

No system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use the Service responsibly and never share sensitive personal information in chat.

9. Children & Minors

ChatNow is strictly intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from or provide the Service to anyone under 18. If you believe a minor is using the Service, please report it immediately to safety@chatnow.com.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — to request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Erasure — to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification — to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Object — to object to processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability — to receive your data in a portable format

Because we collect almost no personal data, most requests can be fulfilled immediately. Contact us at privacy@chatnow.com to exercise any of these rights.

EU/EEA users may also lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority (DPA).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
