Safety Tips

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to have a safe, enjoyable experience on ChatNow.

Updated for 2026 For all users

ChatNow is anonymous — that's part of the fun. But anonymity works both ways. The person on the other side is also anonymous to you. Stay smart, stay safe.

Protect Your Personal Information

Never share sensitive personal details with strangers online, no matter how friendly they seem. This includes:

Your full real name
Home or work address
Phone number
Bank or credit card details
Passwords or PINs
Social media accounts or handles
School or workplace name
Daily routine or schedule

Trust Your Instincts

If a conversation makes you feel uncomfortable, confused, or pressured — disconnect immediately. You don't owe anyone an explanation. Healthy interactions should feel fun and respectful. Warning signs to watch for:

  • Someone pushes you to share personal details too quickly
  • Requests for photos, especially anything revealing
  • Overly intense or romantic language very early in conversation
  • Asking to move the conversation to another platform right away
  • Claiming to need money, gift cards, or cryptocurrency
  • Making you feel guilty for not complying with requests

Remember: you can press Next or close the tab at any time. No questions asked.

Be Careful with Images

Think twice before sending any photos, especially ones that reveal your face, location, or identity. Once an image is sent, you lose control over it. Specifically:

  • Never send explicit or intimate images to someone you don't know
  • Be wary of photos that show identifiable locations (your home, school, street signs)
  • If someone pressures you for photos, report them and disconnect
  • Screenshots can be taken without your knowledge — treat everything as potentially permanent

Recognise Scams & Fraud

Online scammers use chat platforms to target people. Common scam patterns include:

  • Romance scams: Building a fake emotional connection to eventually ask for money
  • Lottery/prize scams: Claiming you've won something but need to pay a fee first
  • Fake emergencies: Pretending to be in crisis and needing immediate financial help
  • Investment fraud: Promising quick returns on crypto or stock tips
  • Phishing links: Sending URLs that steal your login credentials or install malware

If something sounds too good to be true — it almost certainly is. Never send money to someone you've only met online.

ChatNow is 18+ Only

ChatNow is strictly for users who are 18 years of age or older. If you suspect you are speaking with a minor, please report the conversation immediately using the in-chat report button.

If you are under 18, please leave the site. There are age-appropriate platforms designed for you.

Report & Disconnect

You are our most important line of defence against bad actors. Please report any of the following immediately:

  • Harassment, threats, or abusive language
  • Requests for illegal activity
  • Any content involving minors
  • Scam attempts or suspicious behaviour
  • Non-consensual sharing of intimate images

Use the Report button within the chat interface. Reports are reviewed and action is taken promptly. You can also disconnect at any time — your safety always comes first.

Look After Your Wellbeing

Random chatting can be exciting, but it's important to maintain healthy boundaries with your time and emotions. Tips for staying well:

  • Take breaks — don't spend hours in continuous chat sessions
  • Don't let a bad interaction ruin your day. Move on and chat with someone new
  • If you feel anxious or distressed after a chat, talk to someone you trust
  • If you are in crisis or experiencing thoughts of self-harm, please reach out to a professional helpline in your country

Need help? Our Contact page is always open, or visit our Community Rules to understand what behaviour is expected on the platform.
