About ChatNow

We're building a world where anyone, anywhere can make a real human connection — instantly and safely.

Our Mission

ChatNow was built on a simple idea: the best conversations happen between strangers. In a world where social media feeds are curated and algorithms decide who you see, we believe there's something powerful about talking to someone you've never met before — no filters, no follower counts, just two real people connecting.

Our mission is to provide a free, safe, and spontaneous platform for people across the globe to chat, share ideas, make friends, and simply be themselves.

Our Story

ChatNow started in 2022 as a small side project by a team of developers who were frustrated with the state of online social platforms — too much noise, too much data harvesting, and not enough genuine connection.

We launched with a single feature: random text chat. Within weeks, thousands of people were connecting every day from over 50 countries. We listened to our users, iterated quickly, and grew ChatNow into the full-featured platform it is today — complete with group chat rooms, direct messages, and a global community of millions.

Today, ChatNow is one of the most trusted free chat platforms on the web, with users from 190+ countries sending over 50 million messages every month.

Our Values

Privacy First

We don't require accounts, emails, or phone numbers. Your conversations are never stored on our servers. Your data is yours.

Safety Always

We have zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, or illegal content. Our moderation tools and community reporting keep ChatNow a safe space.

Truly Free

ChatNow is and always will be completely free to use. No ads, no premium tiers, no upselling. Just pure chat.

Always Improving

We ship new features every week based on community feedback. If you have an idea, we genuinely want to hear it.
